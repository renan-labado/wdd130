Philippines

Philippines is a country in Southeast Asia. It is divided into three parts; Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Home to the perfect cone volcano, Mount Mayon. Filipinos are known for being hospitable, kind, and generous. Some people consider us as the warmest country because of the said characteristics. There's a lot of great tourist spots here in the Philippines other than Mount Mayon. Boracay Beach, Siargao (Which is a surfing place), and El Nido in Palawan are some places to visit once you come here.
